Privacy & Service Policies

The Leaf Disease Detection System provides AI-based crop disease analysis. This policy explains how data is collected and used to ensure a safe and reliable service for farmers and users.

General Usage Guidelines

  • Upload clear leaf images for accurate detection.
  • Results are advisory and not guaranteed treatments.
  • Consult agriculture experts for severe crop issues.
  • Avoid uploading unrelated or harmful images.

Data Collection

  • Uploaded images may be used to improve AI accuracy.
  • User login information is stored securely.
  • We do not sell personal data.

Service Limitations

  • Accuracy depends on image quality.
  • Internet access is required for detection.

User Responsibility

  • Users decide actions based on AI advice.
  • Safe farming practices should be followed.

Security & Privacy

  • User data is protected using secure technologies.
  • Images are used only for system improvement.

Policy Updates

  • Policies may update as services improve.

Contact & Support

  • Users can contact support for assistance.
